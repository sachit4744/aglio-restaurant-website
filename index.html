Welcome to AGLIO

Authentic Italian Experience

At Aglio, we believe that great Italian cuisine is more than just food – it's a celebration of tradition, family, and the simple pleasure of sharing a meal with loved ones. Our restaurant brings together time-honored recipes with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create an authentic dining experience that transports you straight to Italy.

From our handmade pasta to our wood-fired pizzas, every dish is crafted with passion and attention to detail. We invite you to join us for an unforgettable culinary journey through the flavors of Italy.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Vivek Patel

Founder & Red Seal Certified Chef

Chef Vivek Patel, founder of Aglio, is a Red Seal–certified chef with years of culinary experience and a deep passion for Italian cuisine.

He is dedicated to transforming simple, honest ingredients into elegant dishes that highlight balance, texture, and flavor. Known for his creative approach and high standards, Vivek strives to craft memorable dining experiences that celebrate both tradition and innovation.

With Aglio, he brings together his love of Italian food, hospitality, and artistry to create a restaurant that feels personal, welcoming, and unmistakably his own.

Made Fresh, Made with Love

Every dish at Aglio is prepared with the finest ingredients, sourced locally and imported directly from Italy. Our artisanal approach ensures that each meal is a masterpiece of flavor and presentation.

From our signature handmade pasta to our authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas baked in our wood-fired oven, we bring the true taste of Italy to your table. Our menu features seasonal specialties and timeless classics, all prepared with love and attention to detail.
